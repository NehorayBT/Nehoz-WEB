Enzo Agada 2024


“Enzo Agada” is a grassroots arts festival held annually in Netanya, Israel. It turns everyday spaces—homes, courtyards, and local streets—into live venues for music, theater, and workshops, all led by local artists and residents. The goal is to build community through creativity and accessible culture. As part of the project, I designed the event’s visual identity—including fliers, a printed planner, and large-scale signage—ensuring a cohesive and inviting aesthetic that reflected the festival’s inclusive and artistic spirit.


The designs also included custom postcards for participating artists, neighborhood-specific planners for regional coordination, and branded merchandise such as tote bags and stickers—each element crafted to strengthen the festival’s visual identity and foster a sense of belonging among both creators and attendees.
